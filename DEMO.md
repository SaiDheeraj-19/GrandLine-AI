# 🌊 GrandLine AI — Command Center Tactical Demo

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Mission Readiness Check**: Ensure you have run `node create_users.js` and `node seed.js` (or used the "Seed Demo Data" button in the sidebar) to populate your tactical environment.

---

### 📡 Phase 1: Secure Link Establishment (Login)
1.  Navigate to the **Login Portal**.
2.  Select the **Admin** role — observe the **Access ID** pre-populating with `admin@grandline.ai`.
3.  Click **ENTER COMMAND CENTER**.
4.  *Visual Note*: Notice the high-fidelity scan lines and glassmorphic tactical panel.

### 🛡️ Phase 2: Command Deck Operations
1.  **Situational Awareness**: Observe the central map. Red pulse markers indicate urgent "Threats," while blue zones indicate "Volunteer Coverage."
2.  **Intelligence Feed**: On the left, scroll through the live signals. Click on a high-urgency ticket (e.g., "Flood in Wayanad").
3.  **Deployment**: On the right panel, review the **Tactical Action** recommended by Gemini AI. Click **DEPLOY RESPONSE** to authorize field responders.

### 🤖 Phase 3: ARIA Neural Link (AI Coordination)
1.  Click the **ARIA icon** (bottom-right floating bubble).
2.  **Ask for a Summary**: Type "What are the most critical issues currently across India?"
3.  **Ask for Recommendations**: Type "Which volunteers should I deploy for the Muzaffarpur food shortage?"
4.  *ARIA will cross-reference live Firestore data to give you data-driven coordination advice.*

### 📂 Phase 4: Data Ingestion (Multimodal Feed)
1.  Navigate to **Data Feed** from the sidebar.
2.  **Upload a Signal**:
    *   **Text**: Paste a report like "Heavy rains in Assam causing river overflow near Guwahati."
    *   **Image**: Upload a photo of flood damage.
3.  Observe the **Neural Extraction** process where the AI identifies the latitude, severity, and required skills automatically.
